Registration process

You can register on We Sun Solve! by filling the registration form.
The only information we need is an email address, a login and a password. Please note that at the moment we don't verify correct e-mail addresses, but this will be the case in the future, so please enter a correct email address or you'll risk your account in the future.

There are several feature that are only available to registered users, like:

  • Adapt UI settings of the website;
  • Encode your servers and keep track of their patching level;
  • Setup custom patches list and keep track of their updates;
  • *SOON* Saved search with notifications;
  • *SOON* History of your browsing (patches/bug/searchs);
  • *SOON* Make your list of patches public and let people rate them;
  • *SOON* Vote for other users' custom lists;
  • *SOON* Post public or private comments on bugs/patches;
  • *SOON* Download all the readme's of a patch list
